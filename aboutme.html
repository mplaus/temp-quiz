About The Rundown

The Rundown is a relevant, fun and daily news quiz. The website is accessible to anyone who has an internet connection. It is an innovative way for news junkies and gamers alike to read and understand the news.

Through the quiz format, The Rundown ignites a competitive behavior nestled in the souls of any consumer of the news. With an updated leaderboard and bonus point opportunities, the quiz encourages continued participation over time.

Content included in The Rundown is local, national and global. By combing through the morning news, our team picks pieces to best inform users for their day ahead. Not only do the answers to the news quiz summarize the current events, but they also provide hyperlinks to news outlets for more indepth information.

We hope that in the future The Rundown will be able to increase its usership and better participation through competitions. With sponsored content and buyouts from local businesses, we wish to offer our users gift cards and prizes they will truly appreciate and fight for.
